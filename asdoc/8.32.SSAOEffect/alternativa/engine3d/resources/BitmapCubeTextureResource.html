Classpublic class BitmapCubeTextureResource
InheritanceBitmapCubeTextureResource TextureResource Resource Object

Resource of cube texture. Allows user to upload cube texture, that consists of six BitmapData images to GPU. Size of texture must be power of two (e.g., 256Ñ…256, 12812, 25632).
